How to Get Started with ASIL

1

Create Your Account: Click the Sign Up button at the top right of the page. Fill in your information, including your email, username, and password, to create a personalized account.

2

Tell Us About Your Goals: Complete a quick questionnaire about the bad habit(s) you want to overcome. Share your specific goals, select how long you aim to stay habit-free, and choose a character that represents your journey.

3

Start Your Journey: Once you've completed the setup, you're ready to dive into the ASIL experience. Use our web app to track your progress, stay motivated with your character, and adapt your habits step-by-step!
